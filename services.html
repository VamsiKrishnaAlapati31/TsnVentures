Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes of plots are available?

We offer plots ranging from 100 sq. yards to 500 sq. yards, sold by the cent. Contact us for specific availability in each venture.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible EMI options and bank loan assistance. We work with major banks to ensure easy financing for your purchase.

Are layouts government approved?

Absolutely. All our ventures have proper government approvals, DTCP (Directorate of Town & Country Planning) approved layouts, and clear legal titles.

What is the construction cost per sq. ft?

Construction costs vary based on design, materials, and finishes. Contact us for a personalized quote and we'll provide a detailed estimate within 48 hours.

Can I visit the site before purchasing?

Of course! We encourage site visits. Contact us to schedule a free visit and we'll arrange transportation from nearby towns.
