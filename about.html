Our Story

Building Trust Since 2018

TSN Ventures was founded with a simple yet powerful vision — to make premium housing accessible to every family. Based in Gantavarigudem, Andhra Pradesh, we started by developing our first residential layout and have since grown into one of the region's most trusted real estate developers.

We believe that every family deserves a home in a well-planned community. That's why we go beyond just selling plots — we build entire ecosystems with drainage, water supply, paved roads, parks, and community spaces.

From middle-class families to first-time investors, our ventures are designed to offer the best value for money without compromising on quality or infrastructure.
